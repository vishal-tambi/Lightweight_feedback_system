Privacy Policy

Feedback System - Internal Team Communication Platform

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Feedback System ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our internal team communication platform. This policy applies to all users of the Feedback System, including managers and employees.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, role (manager/employee)
  • Authentication Data: Encrypted passwords and session tokens
  • Profile Information: Manager ID for employees, team relationships

2.2 Feedback Data

  • Feedback Content: Strengths, areas for improvement, sentiment analysis
  • Metadata: Creation dates, modification timestamps, acknowledgment status
  • Relationships: Manager-employee relationships and team structures

2.3 Usage Data

  • Access Logs: Login times, page visits, feature usage
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser information, device details

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide feedback management services
  • Enable secure authentication and authorization
  • Facilitate team communication and performance tracking
  • Generate analytics and insights for managers

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Improve system functionality and user experience
  • Ensure system security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Internal Sharing

  • Managers: Can view feedback they've provided to their team members
  • Employees: Can view feedback they've received from their managers
  • System Administrators: Have access for maintenance and support purposes

4.2 External Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To service providers who assist in system operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure JWT-based authentication system
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and authorization
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery

5.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Feedback data: Retained for 7 years for compliance purposes
  • Log data: Retained for 90 days for security monitoring
  • Deletion: Data is permanently deleted upon account closure

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: View your personal information and feedback data
  • Correction: Update your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Control notification settings
  • Manage email preferences

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • System functionality and performance

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics without explicit consent.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards and security measures

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying in-app notifications

Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
